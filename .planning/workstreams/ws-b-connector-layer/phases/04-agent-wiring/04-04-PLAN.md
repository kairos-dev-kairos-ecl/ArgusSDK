## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-11 | Spoofing | LLM gRPC listener | accept | Local hop auth is out of scope per gen doc (OS perms / app_id registry are future); listener binds loopback by default (127.0.0.1:5002) |
| T-04-12 | Denial of service | IngestBatch | mitigate | MaxRecvMsgSize and MaxConcurrentStreams enforced on the grpc.Server; invalid signals rejected, not panicked |
| T-04-13 | Tampering | proto→signal conversion | mitigate | Minimal validation (SignalID, Layer) before emit; invalid signals counted in RejectedCount and dropped |
| T-04-14 | Information disclosure | EUC ContextJSON | accept | EUC intentionally records connection metadata (ConnectedHost, ProcessName, hashed Username) for Shadow-AI observability; no general capture per package contract |
</threat_model>
